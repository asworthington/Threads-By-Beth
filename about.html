    Hey there! I’m Beth, and I do a little bit of everything!

    I specialize in sewing alterations and embroidery, but I dabble in some private event planning here and there.

    Sometimes you’ll have a question and think to yourself, hmm I wonder who would know about this? Chances are I'll be able to help you or can point you in the right direction of someone who can.
